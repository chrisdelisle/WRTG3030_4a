What is my role currently in University?

Ethical concerns regarding AI span further than just technological considerations. AI’s large fossil fuel use nd potential harm to the job market are just a few of the many concerns regarding AI. Note that these concerns are not just regarding computer scientists, but everyone including you as a college student looking for jobs. AI’s growing popularity has called for institution and industry collaboration. Specifically, companies implementing AI technology have begun creating relationships with institutions to drive research for AI ethics. What does this mean for you?

You can expect that your institution will involve itself in industry collaborations. Volkswagen, for example, collaborated with Stanford to research AI ethics with its use in their cars. If you care about the environment, your ability to land a job, and other ethical concerns with AI, then involve yourself in these collaborations.
